# Workflow: Ingest

## Definition

Ingest is the LLM-wiki operation where a new source is integrated into the maintained wiki.

## Role in LLM-wiki

Ingest updates summaries, concept pages, cross-links, index, and log so the wiki reflects new input.

## Risks

- Overfitting to one source.
- Failing to update related pages.
- Preserving raw material instead of synthesized wiki content.
