# Workflow: Lint

## Definition

Lint is the operation where the LLM checks wiki health.

## Role in LLM-wiki

Lint looks for stale claims, missing cross-links, orphan pages, unresolved contradictions, weak index entries, and source gaps.

## Risks

- Treating lint as cleanup only, rather than theory maintenance.
- Creating pages to satisfy structure without meaningful content.
