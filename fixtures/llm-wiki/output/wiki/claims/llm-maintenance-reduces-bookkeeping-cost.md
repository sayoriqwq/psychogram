# Claim: LLM Maintenance Reduces Bookkeeping Cost

## Claim

LLM maintenance lowers the cost of cross-linking, updating, summarizing, and checking a wiki.

## Why it matters

Humans often abandon wikis because bookkeeping outgrows value. LLMs can handle repetitive maintenance while humans guide meaning and judgment.
