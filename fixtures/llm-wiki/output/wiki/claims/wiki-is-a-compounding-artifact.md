# Claim: Wiki Is a Compounding Artifact

## Claim

A maintained wiki becomes more valuable because structure, links, contradictions, and synthesis accumulate over time.

## Why it matters

The point of LLM-wiki is not just answering the next question; it is preserving useful synthesis so future questions start from a richer artifact.
