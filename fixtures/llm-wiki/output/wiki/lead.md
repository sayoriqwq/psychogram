# Lead

## One-sentence thesis

LLM-wiki is a pattern where an LLM incrementally builds and maintains a persistent Markdown wiki that compounds through sources, questions, answers, and linting.

## What this concept is

LLM-wiki treats the wiki as a maintained intermediate artifact between raw inputs and future answers. The LLM owns the upkeep; the human directs sources, questions, and judgment.

## What this concept is not

It is not one-shot retrieval, temporary chat synthesis, or a passive notes folder.
