# Question: When Is Index Enough?

## Question

When is a manually maintained index enough for finding relevant wiki pages?

## Why this is a question

At small and moderate scale, an index may be sufficient. As the wiki grows, search tooling may become necessary.

## Current answer

An index is enough when important pages are listed with clear one-line descriptions and the wiki scale is still readable by an agent in context.

## Non-answer

"Always build a search engine" is not the answer; tooling should follow need.
