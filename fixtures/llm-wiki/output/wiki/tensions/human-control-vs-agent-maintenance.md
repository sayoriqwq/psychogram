# Tension: Human Control vs Agent Maintenance

## Tension

The human should direct meaning and acceptance, while the agent performs much of the wiki maintenance.

## Pole A

Human control keeps judgment and direction grounded.

## Pole B

Agent maintenance keeps the wiki current at low cost.

## Why they conflict

More autonomy reduces human burden but increases risk of misinterpretation.

## Current resolution

The agent maintains structure and proposes updates; the human remains responsible for direction and acceptance.

## Tradeoff

Some maintenance requires review, but the wiki remains aligned with the human's intent.

## Revisit when

Agent edits repeatedly require correction.
