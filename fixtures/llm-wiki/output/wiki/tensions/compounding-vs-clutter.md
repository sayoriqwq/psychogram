# Tension: Compounding vs Clutter

## Tension

The wiki should grow through useful synthesis, but too much filing creates clutter.

## Pole A

Compounding: useful knowledge should accumulate.

## Pole B

Clutter control: low-value content should not pollute the wiki.

## Why they conflict

The same act of writing back can either preserve value or create noise.

## Current resolution

File back reusable synthesis, not every answer or raw fragment.

## Tradeoff

Some borderline ideas may be lost, but the wiki stays readable.

## Revisit when

Repeated queries need material that was discarded.
