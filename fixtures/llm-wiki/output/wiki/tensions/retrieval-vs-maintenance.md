# Tension: Retrieval vs Maintenance

## Tension

Knowledge systems can retrieve raw material at query time, or maintain synthesized wiki pages ahead of time.

## Pole A

Retrieval keeps source handling flexible and avoids premature synthesis.

## Pole B

Maintenance preserves accumulated synthesis and cross-links.

## Why they conflict

Retrieval defers work until each query; maintenance does work earlier and must keep it current.

## Current resolution

LLM-wiki favors maintaining a persistent wiki while preserving links back to sources.

## Tradeoff

Maintenance creates upkeep obligations but enables compounding value.

## Revisit when

The wiki becomes stale or maintenance cost exceeds answer value.
