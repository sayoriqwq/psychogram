# Workflow: Update Glossary

## Purpose

Maintain this LLM-wiki concept wiki's term contract.

## Steps

1. Add terms that recur or affect interpretation.
2. Keep `Is`, `Is not`, and `Location / Page type` clear.
3. Link expanded explanations to concept pages.
4. Update index/log after substantial glossary changes.

## Done when

- Glossary entries are short, bounded, and consistent with wiki pages.
