# Workflow: Lint

## Purpose

Check this LLM-wiki concept wiki for structural health.

## Checks

- Missing index entries.
- Missing related links.
- Question pages without rationale.
- Tension pages without poles or tradeoff.

- Log gaps after substantial changes.

## Done when

- Clear issues are fixed.
- Ambiguous issues are recorded.
