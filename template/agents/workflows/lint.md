# Workflow: Lint

## Purpose

Check the concept wiki for structural and semantic health.

## Checks

- Orphan pages.
- Pages missing useful Related links.
- Question pages missing a reason why they are real questions.
- Tension pages missing poles, conflict, resolution, tradeoff, or revisit condition.
- Claims with no challenges or related pages.
- Glossary terms missing pages or conflicting with page content.
- Pages missing from `index.md`.
- Substantial changes missing from `log.md`.
- Stale staging content.

## Done when

- Clear issues are fixed.
- Ambiguous issues are recorded as questions or tensions.
- Index/log reflect substantial maintenance.
